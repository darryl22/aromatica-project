OMELETTE OF THE DAY

EXTRAS

SAUSAGES

a variety of sausages such as classic pork, chicken, turkey, or vegetarian options like plant-based sausages. Include choices like links, patties, or bratwursts.

BACON

Serve crispy bacon strips or thick-cut bacon for those who enjoy this breakfast staple. Consider options like regular, smoked, or maple-flavored bacon.

HAM

Add slices of juicy ham to your menu, whether it's honey-glazed, smoked, or Black Forest ham. Offer it as a side or incorporate it into breakfast sandwiches or omelets.

CORNED BEEF HASH

A classic breakfast add-on made with diced corned beef mixed with potatoes, onions, and spices. Served crispy and golden brown.

GRILLED TOMATOES

Offer grilled tomato halves seasoned with herbs or spices as a healthy and flavorful addition to any breakfast plate.

MUSHROOMS

Offer grilled tomato halves seasoned with herbs or spices as a healthy and flavorful addition to any breakfast plate.

BAKED BEANS

Add a British touch to your breakfast menu with a side of savory baked beans cooked with tomato sauce, spices, and sometimes bacon or sausage.

HASH BROWNS

Crispy and golden shredded or diced potatoes seasoned with herbs or spices. Offer options like classic hash browns, loaded with cheese and bacon, or sweet potato hash browns.

TOAST

Provide a choice of bread for toasting, such as white, whole wheat, sourdough, or rye. Offer a selection of spreads like butter, jam, peanut butter, or Nutella.

EXTRA EGGS

Allow customers to add additional eggs to their breakfast orders, whether it's fried, scrambled, poached, or an egg white option.

VEGAN BUDDHA BOWL WITH ROASTED CHICKPEAS AND TAHINI DRESSING

GRILLED SHRIMP AND MANGO SALAD WITH CITRUS DRESSING

A vibrant salad combining grilled shrimp, juicy mango slices, mixed greens, and a tangy citrus dressing for a burst of flavors.

LOBSTER ROLL WITH LEMON HERB BUTTER

A luxurious lobster roll filled with succulent lobster meat tossed in a lemon herb butter, served in a buttery toasted bun.

BEEF TENDERLOIN SLIDERS WITH CARAMELISED ONIONS AND GORGONZOLA
